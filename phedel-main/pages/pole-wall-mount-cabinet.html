Installation Guide

Step-by-step installation process for both mounting configurations

Pole Mount Installation

1

Prepare Mounting Location

Select appropriate pole location and verify pole diameter compatibility (100-300mm).

2

Install Mounting Bands

Position stainless steel mounting bands around pole and secure with anti-rotation brackets.

3

Mount Cabinet

Attach cabinet to mounting brackets and verify level alignment and secure fastening.

4

Cable Entry

Route cables through designated entry points and seal all openings properly.

Wall Mount Installation

1

Mark Mounting Points

Use mounting template to mark drill points on wall surface ensuring proper alignment.

2

Install Wall Brackets

Drill holes and install adjustable wall brackets with appropriate anchors for wall type.

3

Attach Cabinet

Mount cabinet to wall brackets and adjust for level positioning with vibration dampening pads.

4

Final Connections

Complete cable routing, grounding connections, and perform final sealing of all entry points.
