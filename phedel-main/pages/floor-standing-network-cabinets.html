Technical Specifications

Detailed specifications for floor standing network cabinets

Physical Dimensions

Height Options: 18U, 24U, 32U, 42U, 47U
Width Options: 600mm, 800mm (19\")
Depth Options: 600mm, 800mm, 1000mm, 1200mm
Load Capacity: 800kg (1764 lbs) static
Material: Cold-rolled steel, 2.0mm thickness
Finish: RAL 7035 light gray or RAL 9005 black

Advanced Features

Protection Rating: IP20
Cooling System: Forced air circulation with fans
Door Options: Glass, perforated, or solid steel
Access Control: Multi-point locking, key or electronic
Cable Management: Vertical and horizontal cable managers
Standards: IEC 60297, EIA-310-D, ANSI/TIA-942
